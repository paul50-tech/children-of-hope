Our Story

Designed for tastemakers, by tastemakers.

Children of Hope began as a neighborhood collective in Migori Town, where families and makers came together to celebrate everyone equally. We now curate inclusive boutique experiences that champion mindful fashion and storytelling for all, because hope belongs to everyone.

  • Hope for everyone — we champion inclusive collections for all families
  • Ethical sourcing from independent makers
  • Limited drops to keep inventory special yet accessible
